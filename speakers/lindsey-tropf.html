Lindsey Tropf

Immersed Games

Co-Founder and CEO

Lindsey Tropf is Co-founder & CEO of Immersed Games, creating Tyto Online, an online video game for empowering students to deeply learn and engage in STEM. She was previously a doctoral candidate in School Psychology at the University of Florida (all-but-dissertation) with expertise in program evaluation and data-based decision making, which she now uses to lead strategy and product vision at Immersed Games.
