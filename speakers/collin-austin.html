Bio

Collin is the Founder and COE (Chief of Everything) of one of the largest scooter dealerships in the country, New Scooters 4 Less (NS4L). A graduate of the University of Florida, Collin founded the company in 2004 after continuously being met with signs for ‘Full Bus’ while waiting for his transportation. Thirteen years later, Collin has been instrumental in enhancing the way Gainesville commutes. NS4L’s motto is: “Create and recreate the ultimate customer experience.” The company continues to break it's own records and those of other top scooter distributors in the country, has provided a score of jobs, reached millions of dollars in revenue while selling and servicing thousands of scooters. Now NS4L’s mission is to “Make Gainesville the scooter capital of the world”. This year, the team created a new product line called Gainesville Golf Carts after seeing the opportunity to serve a new customer demographic.

Collin became an investor, then a majority stakeholder and eventually sold Gulejo Coffee to help fuel other entrepreneurs and their teams. Collin is an active member in the Gainesville community, through being involved in several mentoring opportunities, has facilitated a group of young CEO’s in an entrepreneur roundtable created by the Chamber, and has served on the Diplomat Team for the Gainesville Area Chamber of Commerce. Collin says: “I serve my most important role as a follower of Jesus Christ, a husband to my wife Shannon, and a father to my two sons, Schaefer and Maddox.”
